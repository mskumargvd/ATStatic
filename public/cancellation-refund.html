How to Request a Refund

  1. Send Email Request: Email us at refunds@agnidhra-technologies.com with your cancellation request.
  2. Provide Details: Include full name, course name, payment ID, and reason for cancellation.
  3. Await Confirmation: We'll review and confirm your refund eligibility within 48 hours.
  4. Receive Refund: Refund will be processed to your original payment method.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Course cancellation request must be submitted in writing via email
  • Original payment receipt or transaction ID must be provided
  • Refund requests must include student's full name and registered email
  • Course materials (if any) must be returned or access revoked
  • Partial completion of course modules may affect refund eligibility

Special Circumstances

Medical Emergency: Full refund available with valid medical documentation, regardless of timing.

Technical Issues: If we cannot provide course access due to technical problems, full refund guaranteed.

Course Cancellation: If we cancel or postpone a course, 100% refund or free transfer to next batch.
